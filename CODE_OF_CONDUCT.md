# Code of conduct

## Our commitment

We commit to making participation in HookShield a harassment-free experience regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience level, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Give and accept technical feedback with empathy.
- Prioritise user safety, privacy, and responsible disclosure.
- Avoid sharing real secrets, personal payloads, or unauthorised security targets.
- Take responsibility, apologise, and repair harm when a mistake occurs.

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, and publishing another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to **jmdcore.dev@gmail.com**. Reports will be reviewed fairly and confidentially. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban contributors whose behaviour is harmful or unsafe.

This policy is adapted from the principles of the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
